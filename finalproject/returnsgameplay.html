RPG Elements

Shadowrun returns is an rpg, split into combat and non-combat sections. It features standard RPG elements, leveling up skills and abilities, skill checks, the whole nine yards.


Non-combat sections

When not in combat, you walk around the world from a top-down perspective. You use this time to talk to npcs about events that recently occured in the story and prepare for the next by purchaing new gear, consumables, augmentations or upgrading your skills.


Combat

When in combat, the game becomes turn based and each of your party memebers, up to 4, have a set amount of action points to use before it becomes the enemies turn. You use your consumables, guns, magic, melee weapons, etc to defeat your opponents. After combat ends, you are returned to the free exploring mode.


Decking

Decking, or hacking, puts the decker into cyberspace, so they can no longer interact with the real world for how long they are there. In cyberspace, they always move as though they are in combat, and combat plays out normally, just instead of guns you use your programs.
