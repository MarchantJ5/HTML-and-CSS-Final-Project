Main Crew


Dietrich

Former punk singer and current shaman of dragonslayer, Dietrich is the reliable old man of the group.


Glory

Glory is the melee specialist and team medic. She is nihilistic adnd distant, carrying a lot of baggage with her.


Blitz

Blitz is the resident decker, egotistical, cowardly, and loaded with debt.


Eiger

Eiger is the no-nonsense markwoman of the team.
